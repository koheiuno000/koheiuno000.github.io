### Project Overview
This research aims to identify factors to improve quality education focusing on teaching and learning processes in developing contries.

### Key Activities & Responsibilities

1. **Fieldwork & Primary Data Collection**
   * Conducted extensive data collection in **Bangladesh and Uganda**.
   * Collected qualitative data including **video-recorded lessons**, student notebooks, and semi-structured teacher interviews.
   * Coordinated school visits and established protocols for on-site research.

2. **Data Analysis**
   * Developed and implemented a **coding system** for the collected video records to quantify and qualify classroom interactions.

3. **Dissemination of Findings**
   * Drafted academic papers and developed presentation materials based on the analyzed data.
   * **International Conference Presentation:** Presented research findings from the Mongolian context at the **Comparative Education Society of Asia (CESA) 2023**.
