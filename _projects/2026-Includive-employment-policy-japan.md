- Conducted a comprehensive review of Japan’s disability-inclusive employment policies, legal frameworks, and institutional arrangements (Policy and institutional analysis).

- Collected, reviewed, and synthesized information from government policies, legislation, administrative statistics, academic literature, and institutional reports (Literature review and evidence synthesis).

- Analyzed key disability employment policy instruments, including the statutory employment quota system, levy and grant system, special subsidiary company system, and reasonable accommodation measures (Policy evaluation and regulatory analysis).

- Assessed vocational rehabilitation services, employment transition support, employment retention support, and other labor market inclusion mechanisms for persons with disabilities (Labor market and social inclusion analysis). 

- Reviewed school-to-work transition pathways and support systems linking the education, welfare, and labor sectors (Education-to-employment transition analysis).

- Examined disability-inclusive employment initiatives implemented by government agencies, local authorities, employers, and support organizations (Stakeholder and institutional mapping).

- Conducted quantitative analysis of disability employment indicators, including employment rates, labor market participation, employment retention, and utilization of employment support services (Statistical analysis and monitoring framework assessment).

- Compiled and visualized statistical data through analytical tables, charts, and figures to support evidence-based policy analysis (Data analysis and reporting)

- Identified strengths, challenges, and good practices within Japan’s disability employment system (Policy analysis)

- Developed policy recommendations related to employment equity, institutional coordination, and inclusive labor market participation (Policy recommendation development).

- Prepared and submitted an approximately 80-page English-language final report, including policy analysis, institutional mapping, statistical analysis, and recommendations (Technical report writing).

- Coordinated with the client throughout the review, revision, and finalization process (Client communication and stakeholder coordination).
